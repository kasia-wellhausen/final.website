Adult Group Coaching


Every week, we provide coaching to a diverse group of adults in our various sessions. No matter how busy your day may be, we offer flexible time slots that allow you to choose the most convenient session for you. Participating in group lessons not only allows you to enhance your tennis skills but also provides the valuable opportunity to engage with players of similar standard. This collaborative learning environment not only improves your tennis abilities but also expands your network within the tennis community. Embrace the benefits of learning and growing alongside peers, as it brings both personal development and a wider tennis network to your journey.


Individual Coaching


Whether you're seeking targeted instruction or aiming to elevate your skills to new heights, our individual coaching sessions are tailored to meet your specific needs. Regardless of your proficiency level, be it a beginner or a competitive player, our one-on-one coaching is designed to be highly rewarding. We offer comprehensive guidance in all facets of tennis, encompassing technique, strategy, physical fitness, and the psychological aspects of the game. By focusing solely on your development, we ensure a personalized approach that maximizes your potential and accelerates your progress on the court. With our individual coaching, you'll receive the dedicated attention and expertise necessary to excel in every aspect of the game at the time that suit you. If you are unsure of your level or which class to choose please call or text on 07792 629093 or email ernies_extremetennis@hotmail.com.




Junior Coaching


Group Coaching


Our bustling group coaching program brings together approximately 100 junior players each week, creating a vibrant and energetic atmosphere on the courts. Tailored for young tennis enthusiasts, our junior group lessons take place after school and over the weekends, ensuring convenient scheduling for busy families. These dynamic sessions provide an ideal platform for young players to put their skills to the test against peers of similar levels. Within this enjoyable yet challenging environment, juniors not only improve their tennis abilities but also cultivate self-assurance and foster lasting friendships with fellow tennis enthusiasts. Join us in creating unforgettable experiences, building confidence, and nurturing lifelong connections with like-minded players. Get in touch for specific days and times to be a part of our thriving junior group coaching sessions.
